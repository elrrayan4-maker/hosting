FAQ

Common Questions

How fast will my server be set up?
Your server is deployed and live within 60 seconds of completing your order. Our automated provisioning handles everything — no manual setup, no waiting, no technical knowledge required.
Can I install mods, plugins, and custom content?
Yes. We support one-click installation for all major platforms — Forge, Fabric, Paper, Spigot, Oxide/uMod, SourceMod, BepInEx, FiveM resource management, and more. You also get full FTP/SFTP access to upload files manually.
What is your refund policy?
We offer a 72-hour money-back guarantee on all new orders. If you're not satisfied for any reason, contact our support team within 72 hours and we'll process a full refund. No questions asked.
Is DDoS protection included for free?
Yes, all servers include enterprise-grade DDoS mitigation up to 1Tbps at no extra cost. Our multi-layer protection system automatically detects and filters malicious traffic before it reaches your server, keeping your players connected.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Absolutely. You can scale your resources up or down at any time through your control panel or by opening a support ticket. Plan changes are applied within minutes and your data is always preserved.
Do you support server clustering (ARK, Minecraft networks)?
Yes, we fully support multi-server clustering for ARK Survival, Minecraft BungeeCord/Velocity networks, and other cluster setups. Our support team can help you configure cross-server connections. Open a ticket to get started.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and cryptocurrency. All payments are processed securely via SSL encryption.
